Segmentation of Brain Glioma Tumor from MRI slices

I compared several methods of segmenting tumors including both traditional methods like active contour, or Otsu thresholding. I also trained a standard U-Net and then modified the architecture to integrate transformer into it to create a TransUNet as deep learning methods.

Comparison between Performances of Standard Agent and Agent with Plan-and-Act Pipeline on Long-horizon Tasks

We tested both approaches with a serie of long-horizon tasks in Webarena virtual environment and analyzed the failure rates of the two approaches and failure types for each failed task in each approach. We then compared the analysis to judge whether Plan-and-Act approach has true improvements based on standard agent.
